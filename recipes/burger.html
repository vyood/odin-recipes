Burger recipe

Burger

A dish consisting of a round patty of ground beef, or sometimes another savory ingredient, that is fried or grilled and typically served in a split bun or roll with various condiments and toppings.

Ingredients

  • bun
  • ground beef
  • tomato
  • mayo
  • lettuce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. patty out your ground beef into circle disks about four inches wide
  2. slice all fresh ingredients, such as cheese, lettuce, and tomatoe
  3. butter a pan on medium to high heat and add the patty, cooking about 3-4 min per side until griddled to your liking
  4. remove cooked patty and butter the buns and put them in the pan until golden brown
  5. spread mayo or codiment of your preference onto the bottom toasted bun
  6. assemble your buger with ingredients anyway you choose
