Taco recipe

Taco

A taco is an internationally popular hand-sized food item of Mexican origin combining seasoned meat, vegetables,and other fillings and served inside a folded or rolled corn or flour tortilla.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • chile seasoning
  • shredded cheese
  • shredded lettuce
  • diced tomatoes
  • sour cream
  • hard shell tortilla

Steps

  1. add the ground beef to a pan over meadium heat
  2. cook ground beef until brown and then add chile seasoning
  3. once seasoning is fully incorporated turn off the heat
  4. bake taco shells in the oven for 5 min on 400 degrees
  5. assemble taco with ground beef on the base layer of the shell
  6. top the ground beef with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes and sour cream
